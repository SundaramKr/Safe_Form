Our Mission

Safe Form is an easy-to-use service based platform made for teachers, schools and colleges who conduct their tests/quizes via Google Forms. Our goal is to stop the usage of unfair means by students in a test which is meant to assess their academic caliber. Our platform ensures that the student displays what he has learnt in the test without getting aid from a third party search engine like Google or Bing.

Our Vision

My main aim while making this platform was that it shouldn't be a hassle for the teachers who are already using Google Forms and who find it hard switching platforms. With our unique tab change detection and session termination technology, we aim to empower online education by securing quizes, tests and examinations which are the fundamental criterias of assessing a student's academic growth.
